Our Core Services

Mahajyoti Coaching

Guidance and resources provided by Mahajyoti for competitive exams like UPSC, MPSC, JEE, NEET, and other state/national level exams, ensuring inclusive and affordable learning opportunities.

BARTI Coaching

Special training initiatives by Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Research & Training Institute (BARTI) focused on empowering students from marginalized communities for UPSC, MPSC, and other competitive examinations.

SARTI Coaching

Structured programs by SARTI that emphasize skill development, exam preparation, and career guidance for students aspiring to excel in civil services and state-level competitive exams.

UPSC (Pre/Mains) Exam Coaching

Comprehensive coverage of the UPSC syllabus, focusing on General Studies, CSAT, optional subjects, and essay writing with regular mock tests.

MPSC (पूर्व/मुख्य) परीक्षा Coaching

Specialized, bilingual (English/Marathi) training for MPSC exams, emphasizing state-specific topics and proven preparation strategies.

Rajyaseva Pariksha Preparation

Holistic guidance for the Maharashtra State Services Examination, led by expert faculty with deep domain knowledge.

PSI/STI/Asst./Saral Seva Bharti

Targeted programs including physical fitness guidance and interview simulations for police and administrative service roles.

Comprehensive Test Series

Rigorous mock tests in both online and offline modes to benchmark your preparation and refine your exam-taking strategy.

Flexible Batch Options

Choose from Foundation Batches (10-12 months) for beginners or Weekend Batches for working professionals to fit your schedule.
