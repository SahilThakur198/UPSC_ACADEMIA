Our Services

UPSC (Pre/Mains) Exam Coaching

In-depth coverage of the Union Public Service Commission examinations, including Preliminary (objective-type) and Mains (descriptive) stages. Focus on General Studies, CSAT, optional subjects, and essay writing, with regular mock tests.

MPSC (पूर्व/मुख्य) परीक्षा Coaching

Specialized training for Maharashtra Public Service Commission exams, bilingual (English/Marathi), emphasizing state-specific topics and comprehensive preparation strategies.

Rajyaseva Pariksha Preparation

Comprehensive guidance for Maharashtra State Services Examination with expert faculty and proven methodologies for success.

PSI/STI/Asst./Saral Seva Bharti Coaching

Targeted programs including physical fitness and interview simulations for police and administrative service positions.

Test Series

Rigorous mock tests in online/offline modes to assess your preparation and improve performance through regular practice.

Flexible Batch Options

Foundation Batches (10-12 months for beginners) and Weekend Batches (for working professionals) to suit your schedule.
